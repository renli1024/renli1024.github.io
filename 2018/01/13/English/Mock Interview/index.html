Mock Interview

  • 2018-01-13

考官说话有点快，鼻音有点重，没听清多问问。
面试大概10Min，20-25个问题。
说的时候要看考官的眼神，是否想让你接着说下去。

1、Are you available at the moment?
Yes, I am.

2、What programme did you study in China?/What course have you studied?
The program I studied in China was Computer Science and Technology.
(And I have studied many computer related courses like Operating System, Computer Organization and so on.)
学习的项目(Project)：Study Abroad

3、Which university did you graduate from?
I graduated frome Wuhan University of Technology.

4、What course have you applied?
I have applied Computing Programme.
Why did you applied this programme?
Firstly, I want to be employeed as data analysist in the Internet company, and this programme can provide me a broad range of knowledge for this job.
Secondly, my programme in China was Computer Science and Technology, and it is closely related to this programme.

5、Do you know how long your course duration is?
My course duration is ten monthes.

6、When will the course start?
My course will start on seventeenth of September, twenty eighteen.
(And it will end on twenty-sixth of July, twenty nighteen.)
(2018.9.17-2019.7.26)

7、What is the structure of your course?
I will study one compulsory course named Major Project and four optional courses which are Big Data, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, GPU Programming and Advanced Web Development.
(After finishing these courses, I will get one hundred and twenty credits.)

8、How is your course assessed(评估)?/Is the entire course assessed in the same way?
My courses are assessed in different way. For example, Major Project will be assessed by dissertation, presentation and “question and answer”.
And Big Data, GPU Programming will be assessed by essay and written exam.

9、Do you know anyone who has already studied there?(有没有认识的同学)
No, I don’t know.

10、重要：Why did you choose this course and how does it relate to your previous study?
Firstly, I can learn a broad range of knowledge from these courses, providing the foundation for my later study.
Secondly, through the programming courses like GPU programming, Major Project, I can develop the software solution skill and programming ability, which are highly regarded by employers.
Thirdly, the course is closely relate to my previous study. My program in China was Computer Science and Technology.
(And it will lay a solid foundation for my courses in UWTSD. 和之前的专业有什么关系)

11、What qualification(文凭) are you looking forward/expecting from this program?
I will not get any qualification. Because what I applied is an exchange program.

12、What’s your career aspiration? What’s your intention to do in the future?
My career aspiration is being a data analyst in the Internet company such as Google, Tencent, Alibaba, and so on.
(Because I both like computer science and data science, so I think it’s a good job for me to fulfill my values in society.)

13、重要：Do you have any arrangement for the accommodation? / Which hotel are you looking for? / Where do you intend to live?
I intend to live in the student apartment.
(It is located on one hundred and sixty(160) Helens Road, Swansea SA1 4DQ.)

14、重要：How will you rent a house? / Do you know the name of the agency? / How much is it?
I rent the house by angency.
The agency’s name is yingxuan.
The rent is four hundreds and sixty pounds a month(460磅).

15、How far is your accommodation from the university?
About ten minutes on foot.

16、What factors are you looking forward when you choose your accommodation?
Factors I’m looking forward are security and location.

17、Dou you have enough money for your studies in UK? Is the money under your name?
Yes, I have enough money to support my study. (one hundred and eighty thounds yuan)
It is under my name.

18、How will you support yourself financially?
My parents support me.

19、What your parents do for a living?
My mother is a doctor and my father is a company employee.
家庭年收入：My family’s annual income is about three hundred thounds yuan.
从事了多长时间：20 years.
父母具体职业：
My mother is a doctor, and she helps the disabled to restore their health.
My father works as marketing department manager of the company, and he formulates the development strategy of the company.

20、Is it your father/mother’s own or he/she runs with other partners?
开公司的问题，with other partners.

21、Which department do you intend to work in the future?
I want to be employed in the data analysis department of Internet company, like Google, Tencent, Alibaba and so on.

22、How will the communication skills help you to communicate with customers?（课程怎么帮助你的职业规划，How will the courses help you?）
For example, through Big Data module I can learn mathematics and statistics knowledge, which are necessary to support my later scientific research.
And by learning Advanced Web Development module, I can develop my programming skill and cooperative ability.

23、Have you been to the UK previously?
No, I’ve not.

24、重要：Why do you want to study in the UK rather than other countries? / What are the benefits of studying in the UK compared to your home country or other country?
First of all, UK is an advanced country and has high level of education, which can give me a valuable opportunity to learn and grow.
Secondly, Uk is one of the safest countries in the world, so I’m able to concentrate fully on my study.
Thirdly, I like playing soccer very much. So I want to have a ringside view of UK Premier League, which is one of the best leagues in the world.

25、What’s your intention to do in the UK and why?
I go to UK to study. It will be helpful for me to get a job in China.

26、重要：Why did you choose to study in UWTSD?
Firstly, UWTSD has high academic level. It ranks seventh in UK for academic experience, so I can receive a good education.
Then, it’s Student Satisfaction also ranks top fifteen in UK, which proves that teachers there are very responsible and can give students timely feedback.
Thirdly, UWTSD has established a good cooperative relationship with WUT. So there are many students from WUT are studying there, who can give me lot’s of help in life and study.
How did you find out about it?
My teacher recommeded it to me, and I also get some information on the Internet by myself.

27、重要：What modules are you going to study?
I will study six modules, including one compulsory course named Major Project and four optional courses which are Big Data, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, GPU Programming and Advanced Web Development, all computer-related courses.

28、What’s your course level/academic level?
level 6.

29、重要：How will doing this course help you with your future career plans?
For example, through Big Data module I can learn mathematics and statistics knowledge, which are necessary to support my later scientific research.
And by learning Advanced Web Development module, I can develop my programming skill and cooperative ability.
Besides, learning in UK can also improve my English ability, especially for communicating and writting.

30、重要：How much is your tuition fee?
The tuition fee is ten thousands and five hundred ponds(10500磅).

31、Do you need a meet and greet services?（接机服务）
Yes, I need. And my agent will help me with it.

32、What facilities are you expecting at university? What facilities do you know at university?
caffe, reception, computer room

33、重要：How much is your living expenses?
one thound and fifteen pounds per month(1015).

34、Have you been coached/trained for the interview?
No, I haven’t.

35、Do you have any questions for me?
No, I don’t.

36、Do you plan to work in UK?
No, because after I finish my course in UK, I have to come back to China to continue my rest courses in WUT.

37、What do you plan to do after you finish your course in UK?
和36一样

38、What is your date of birth?
Nineteenth of November, nineteen ninety-six(1996).

39、Do you know your passport number?
Sorry, I don’t know. I forget it.

40、What is your home country address?
Honghe Garden District, No.27 Dianchang Road, Zhengzhou City, Henan Province.

学校全称：
UWTSD, University of Wales Trinity Saint David
WUT, Wuhan university of Technology

学校的四个校区(campus)：
Swansea, Carmarthen, Lampeter and Cardiff.

在哪个校区学习：
Swansea campus.

斯旺西在哪个conutry：
It is located in south west of Welsh.

新校区的具体位置：
新校区具体位置？

申请的专业到底是什么？
Computing programme or Faculty of Architecture Computing and Engineering programme?

course/program/major都是指专业，module指学的课程。
